# SaTML 2026 Accepted Papers - Comprehensive Deep Dive

This document provides a detailed analysis of all accepted papers from SaTML 2026, organized by theme and with targeted perspectives for different stakeholder groups.

## Research Paper Categories

### 1. AI Fingerprinting & Attribution Security
- Smudged Fingerprints: Robustness of AI image fingerprinting in adversarial settings
- Are Robust LLM Fingerprints Adversarially Robust: Analysis of LLM fingerprinting security

### 2. Privacy Attacks & Membership Inference
- Privacy Risks in Time Series: Membership inference in forecasting models
- FedSpy-LLM: Data reconstruction from gradients in federated LLMs
- DP Forest Reconstruction: Testing privacy effectiveness in Random Forests
- DeepLeak: Hardening model explanations against leakage
- Textual Sanitization Evaluation: Privacy risks in text sanitization

### 3. LLM Security & Safety
- Certifiably Robust RAG: Defense against retrieval corruption attacks
- Defeating Prompt Injections: CaMeL framework for prompt injection protection
- Targeting Alignment: Extracting safety classifiers from aligned LLMs
- BinaryShield: Privacy-preserving threat intelligence
- DataFilter: Data contamination in large language models

### 4. Model Robustness & Verification
- Optimal Robust Recourse: Lp-bounded model change for robustness
- Cascading Robustness Verification: Multi-scale robustness analysis
- RobustBlack: Black-box robustness testing
- TEA Targeted Attacks: Targeted adversarial attacks and defenses

### 5. Federated Learning Security
- FedSpy-LLM: Data reconstruction from gradient in federated LLMs
- Contribution Evaluation Fragility: Distortion in federated learning contribution scoring
- CPCL with DP: Constrained private collaborative learning

### 6. Model Unlearning & Privacy
- Deep Unlearning in LLMs: Comprehensive fact removal in language models
- Exact Unlearning via Model Merging: SIFT-Masks for one-shot unlearning
- Gauss-Newton Unlearning: Unlearning with second-order methods
- Oblivious ERT Unlearning: Encrypted exact unlearning

### 7. Physical & Systems Security
- Kraken: EM side-channel attacks for model stealing
- Temporal Misalignment Attacks: Time-based vulnerabilities in ML systems
- CHAI Embodied AI Hijacking: Physical hijacking of AI systems

### 8. Fairness & Explainability
- Homophily-aware Fair GNN: Fair graph neural networks
- DeepLeak Explanations: Privacy in model explanations
- Near-OOD Detection: Out-of-distribution detection methods

### 9. Efficiency & Optimization
- Efficient DP-SGD: Scalable private deep learning
- StriaNet: Fast secure inference with Homomorphic Encryption
- Wasserstein Perturbations: Global explainability framework
- Efficient SSAT: Latent clustering for adversarial training

### 10. Enterprise AI Security
- Adversarial News in Trading: Manipulating LLM-driven algorithmic trading
- C-Level GenAI Lessons: Enterprise perspectives on AI security
- Transfer Learning Reconstruction: Privacy risks in transfer learning

### 11. Dataset Quality

- Beyond TESSERACT: Dataset curation for Android malware classifiers
