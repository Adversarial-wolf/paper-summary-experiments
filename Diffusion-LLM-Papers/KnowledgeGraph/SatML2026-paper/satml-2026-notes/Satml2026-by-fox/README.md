# SaTML 2026 Papers Organization

This directory contains a comprehensive organization of the accepted papers from SaTML 2026, structured for different stakeholder groups.

## Directory Structure

- `papers/` - Individual paper files and abstracts
- `notes/` - Detailed technical notes and analysis
- `diagrams/` - Mermaid diagrams and visualizations
- `mindmaps/` - Mindmap files for different audience groups
- `audience-explanations/` - Explanations tailored for specific stakeholder groups
- `downloads/` - Downloaded papers from arXiv

## Content Organization

### Papers by Category
Papers are organized into 11 main categories:
1. AI Fingerprinting & Attribution Security
2. Privacy Attacks & Membership Inference
3. LLM Security & Safety
4. Model Robustness & Verification
5. Federated Learning Security
6. Model Unlearning & Privacy
7. Physical & Systems Security
8. Fairness & Explainability
9. Efficiency & Optimization
10. Enterprise AI Security
11. Dataset Quality

### Stakeholder Perspectives
Three distinct perspectives are provided:
- **Data Scientists**: Technical methodologies, implementation details, and experimental results
- **Compliance Officers**: Regulatory considerations, privacy mechanisms, and compliance frameworks
- **Executives**: Business risk assessment, strategic investments, and governance frameworks

## Usage

This organization allows users to quickly access relevant information based on their domain expertise or stakeholder role. The mindmaps provide visual overviews, while the detailed explanations offer targeted insights for different audiences.
